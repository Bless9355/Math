    Consider a cube of dimension of $2$-by-$2$-by-$n$. Let $C_n$ The number of ways to 
    perfectly tile this cube with $2$-by-$2$-by-$1$ cubes. Assuming no rotational similairity, the seqeunce 
    $C_n$ is determined entirely by the following recurrence relation. 
    \begin{eqnarray}
        C_1 \ =\ 2, C_2 \ =\ 9 \\ 
        C_n \ = \ 2 C_{n - 1} + 5 C_{n-2}
    \end{eqnarray}
\end{conjecture}
